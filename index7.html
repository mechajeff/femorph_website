Redefining accuracy in digital twin creation

What is FEMORPH?

FEMORPH transforms raw 3D scans into exact replicas of as-manufactured components, enabling high-precision modeling and simulation.

Features

  • High-precision mesh comparison
  • Digital twin validation
  • Simulation-ready integration

FEMORPH uses advanced morphing kernels to maintain element quality during deformation. The software also supports automated Python-driven workflows and batch operations.
